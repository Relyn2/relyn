The Struggles of a Poor Student

Every day, I wake up before sunrise, not because I want to, but because I have to. The long walk to school is my morning routine, my feet tracing the familiar paths of sacrifice. With an empty stomach and a heart full of dreams, I sit in class, absorbing knowledge like a sponge, hoping that education will be my escape from hardship. The nights are long, lit only by a dim candle as I study, pushing through exhaustion, driven by the hope of a better future.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world." – Nelson Mandela
"Success is no accident. It is hard work, perseverance, learning, studying, and sacrifice." – Pelé
